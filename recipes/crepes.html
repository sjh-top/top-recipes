Basic Crepes

Description

These are delicious french style crapes that can easily be made from home. Light, airy and perfect morning snacks!

I generally make these crepes on a professional crepe maker but that doesn't need to be the case. In the past, I have made them in a simple skillet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 teaspon salt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter, melted

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Gradually add in the milk and water, stirring to combine. Add the salt and butter; beat until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan with a circular motion so that the batter coats the surface evenly.
  3. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is light brown. Loosen with a spatula, turn and cook the other side. Serve ho
