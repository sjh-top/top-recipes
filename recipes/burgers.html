Simple Hamburgers

Description

Hamburgers are an exceptionally easy meal to make at home for yourself and the family!

I prefer to make my burgers on my hardwood charcoal fired Big Green Egg but any grill or even a skillet will do.

This recipe will produce four hamburgers

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 1 onion chopped into slices
  • 1 head of lettuce split into leaves
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Start your grill or oven to medium heat. I usually shoot for around 300-400F
  2. Split the beef into 4 equal sections and pat into rounds. Place each on on the grill or skillet till browned. Flip after 2-3 minutes
  3. Place base of burger bun on the plate and follow with the finished meat, lettuce, onion and a dab of ketchup. Put the bun top on and enjoy!
